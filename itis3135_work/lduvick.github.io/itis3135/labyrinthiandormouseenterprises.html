About the Company

Labyrinthian Dormouse Enterprises is a small graphic design company that started in 2019 based in Charlotte, North Carolina and has grown to become a closely knit family enterprise. We have assisted many notorious clients such as Colin Mochrie and Ryan Stiles with creating a website that speaks to their audience. We are originating from our two founders Bergs Bonni and Tasmina Devolo who started working as contracters and moved on to successfully make their own buisness which has lead to our current position that we have today. Labyrinthian Dormouse Enterprises strives to obtain nationwide clientele while still maintaining the small family built atmosphere that made us successful at the beginning.

  • We specialize in graphic design and also assist with UI as well.
  • We do not contract out any help, so all work is done by our homegrown staff.
  • All work is gone over meticulously through several different QA layers to ensure the best quality for our clients possible.
  • Our promise to our clients is that every step of the process will be gone over with them to ensure they know where they are in the process
  • Even the workplace we create is always warm and welcoming to all of our employees to ensure everyone enjoys what they do to the fullest.

We are able to create website with multi-functional purposes, that are appealing to your personal audience and still contain your flair whatever it might be. Each website is designed with you in mind and maximizes the amount of input that you give us. We can also design websites that can be for personal use as well as corporate use, whether you are trying to build your own small buisness or are a corporation needing a new website.
